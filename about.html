WHO WE ARE


We, at Chawla Bags have been fashioning Bags, for well over 20 years, for the style-conscious people around the globe.

We started our journey as a small Retail shop in Sanganer, Jaipur. Today, we are manufacturing our own brands and re-creating fashion defination in Bag Industry. We are the most trusted and famous shop in our Region.
